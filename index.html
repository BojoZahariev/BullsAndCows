Bulls and Cows

Wanna play a game?

We will both pick a 4-digit secret number. The digits must be all
different. Then, in turn, we'll try to guess each other's number
and give the number of matches. If the matching digits are in
their right positions, they are "bulls",if in different positions,
they are "cows".

For example:

  • My secret number: 4271
  • You guess: 1234
  • I'll tell you: 1 bull and 2 cows. (The bull is "2", the cows are "4" and "1".)

The first one to reveal the other's secret number wins the game.
